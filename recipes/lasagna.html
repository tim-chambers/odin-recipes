Dad's Lasagna

This is tasty classic italian, best served with salad and breadsticks.

My dad makes this and it's the shit.

This lasagna recipe calls for layers of noodles, meat sauce, cheese, and anything else you feel like throwing in. Bake for a number of minutes in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Cook Meat Sauce
  3. Mix cheeses in bowl
  4. Layer each of the above
  5. Cover in more cheese
  6. Bake until golden brown on top
